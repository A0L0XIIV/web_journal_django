Who am I?

My name is Baran Kaya. I was born in 1994 in Eskişehir/Turkey. I lived in Eskişehir until I moved to Istanbul for a university degree. I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Engineering from Istanbul Technical University (ITU). I graduated from ITU in 2018 and started to work in Huawei Turkey R&D Center. After 11 months, I left my job and started to study for my graduate degree at McMaster University in Hamilton/Canada. I graduated from McMaster University with a Master of Engineering degree in Computer and Software (CAS). I am currently working at Providius as a Software Engineer.

After graduating with my B.Sc. degree in August 2018, I started to work in Huawei Turkey R&D Center. My role was Assistant Software Engineer and my department was Wireless DC. Our projects were mostly focused on Base/Cell Station Management and Monitor Systems. My group was working on an indoor station's software. We mostly focused on bug fixing and some requirement additions. The system was a web project that uses a Java applet in the browser. The server-side was written in C but we mostly worked on the client which is Java and JavaScript. Later, we started to work on a secondary project. It was an internal website project for individual goals. We designed the system from zero and we used Angular in the frontend, Java Spring in the backend and PostgreSQL as a database. I mostly worked on database design and we started to implement the project as a team. But then other main projects blocked our secondary project and it stopped. After 11 months of Huawei's journey, I left my job for graduate study. After graduating with my master's degree currently, I am working at Providius as a Software Engineer. We are developing software for live TV broadcasting.
